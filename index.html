ABOUT US

Shri M.P. Sonwane English Medium School is operated under Sanskar Bahuddeshiya Sanstha. This organization was founded by our chairman Mr. Sanjay Sonwane in 2004 with an objective of giving back to his village Waghali.

Shri M.P Sonwane English MEdium school was established in 2010 in a historic house with around 109 children from 7 villages around. The school strated with 4 classes Nursery, Junior KJ, Senor KG and 1st std and a staff of 5 people. After changing location from 2 wadas, the school finally got its own building on 13th June, 2016 with 11 rooms to start with. Every year one standard incremented as and when the students moved on to a new grade.

Today in 2018 , we have standards till 8th with 228 children from 9 villages.

VISION

Pooerst of Poor children of farmers become independent in every filed and come back to the village to contibute towards the development of their village.

MISSION

Provide students from rural India, quality education with practical knowledge so that they have equal opportunity comapred to the urban children.
